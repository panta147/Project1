Our Services

Education Counselling

Dream Abroad Education and Visa Services have well trained and expert staff who have good knowledge of education counselling. All our education counsellors have government certificate of recognition. Education counselling to students is provided free of cost. Our education counsellor goes through all the latest training and information available to keep them up to date and to provide students with genuine information.

University/College Admission

Dream Education and Visa Services is partner with hundreds of Universities/Colleges from different countries. There are number of option available to our students depending upon their qualification, English language test, financial background etc. This option will be provided to you by our education counsellor, so that students can shape their future towards right path. Also, after providing with option, they will also help you choose the best among them.

Visa lodgement

Assisting with all the process visa lodgement is final process where people must wait for visa decision. After the visa is lodged, we all must wait for good news to come and have patience.

Ongoing assistance after departure

After the visa is granted, Dream Abroad Education and Visa Services job is not finished. We try to make sure that our students have proper support when they reach their destination. We try to provide them with finding accommodation, airport pickup and in some case jobs as well.

GTE preparation

GTE full form is The Genuine Temporary Entrant as per Department of Home Affairs. It is one of the most important tools in granting the visa. It is as powerful as Statement on Purpose. Our experts will help every student in filling this GTE form.

Documentation

Documentation in another important part of application. There are many documents which needs to be attached before any visa is lodge. Documentation plays vital role in visa decision. Our education counsellor will provide you with all the information and sample which students need to make.
